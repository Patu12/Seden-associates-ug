Frequently Asked Questions

What are the prerequisites for joining the CPA classes? ▼

Typically, candidates should hold a bachelor's degree in accounting or a related field. Specific requirements may vary by state board of accountancy. We recommend checking with your state's board for the most accurate information.

How long does it take to complete the CPA program? ▼

The duration depends on your chosen learning path (online vs. physical) and your study pace. Most students aim to complete all four parts of the CPA exam within 12-18 months. Our flexible programs are designed to accommodate various schedules.

What support do you offer for exam preparation? ▼

We provide comprehensive study materials, practice exams, mock tests, one-on-one tutoring sessions (upon request), and ongoing support from our experienced instructors. Our goal is to equip you with all the tools needed to pass your exams confidently.

Are your instructors certified CPAs? ▼

Yes, all our instructors are highly experienced Certified Public Accountants with extensive knowledge in their respective fields and a passion for teaching. They bring real-world insights to the classroom, enriching your learning experience.
